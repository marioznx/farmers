Yes, Let’s Meet at The Market!

A visit to the Charlottetown Farmers Market is a beloved Saturday morning ritual only Islanders know, but visitors soon come to discover. It is the biggest farmers market in PEI — over 60 local vendors — and the only place you can hand-pick fresh produce from Island farms, enjoy a delicious lunch, acquire unique artisan ware, and find tasty treats for the kids.

More than shopping, the Charlottetown Farmers Market is a popular gathering place and living proof of the community ties that make PEI so special. There are more smiles and conversations exchanged at the Market on Saturdays than nickels and dimes. If you haven’t been recently, now is the time to discover new vendors and say hello to old favourites.

Shop local online

So everyone could enjoy fresh local food during the pandemic, our online marketplace was launched in May 2020. Response was incredible and gave vendors much needed support during the lockdown. While the Charlottetown Farmers Market has fully reopened, www.cfm2go.ca continues to offer online shopping and free pick-up on Thursdays.
